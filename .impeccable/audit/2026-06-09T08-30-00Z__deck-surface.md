### P2

- Location: src/App.css line 124, App.tsx meta-row
- Category: Responsive / Accessibility
- Dimensions: 49×21px (height is 48% of 44px minimum)
- Impact: Hard to tap reliably on mobile — this button is the key "jump back to today" affordance

### P3

**[P3] todayPulse animates box-shadow**
- Location: src/App.css line 602–608 (.day-chip.today:not(.active))
- Category: Performance
- Impact: box-shadow forces CPU repaint every frame of the 2.4s infinite pulse animation
- Recommendation: Replace with opacity-pulsed outline or ::after pseudo-element (transform + opacity only)
- Suggested command: /impeccable optimize

**[P3] theme-toggle button 42×42px**

- Suggested command: /impeccable polish
