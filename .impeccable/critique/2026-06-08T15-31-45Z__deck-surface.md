## Anti-Patterns Verdict

LLM assessment: Not AI-flagged overall. Near-black body (#08080f) avoids cream/beige trap. No gradient text, numbered eyebrows, or hero-metric grid. Entree-first hierarchy is a genuine editorial choice. The one tell: pervasive backdrop-filter on every card where the underlying surface has no visual complexity to blur.

Deterministic scan: 1 finding — bounce-easing at src/App.css:196. chipSpring uses cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1), an overshoot spring curve.

## Overall Impression

Well-built utility with strong hierarchy and serious accessibility work. The deck surface mostly disappears into the task. Held back by sub-11px text failing at zoom, decorative glassmorphism with GPU cost and no visual gain, and spring easing that fights the calm personality.

## What's Working

1. Entree-first hierarchy — featured card treatment for the main dish is the right editorial bet.
2. Accessibility depth — tablist/tab/tabpanel ARIA chain complete, arrow-key wrap, aria-label on countdown, aria-hidden on decorative emoji.
3. Skeleton + cached-first loading — invisible in the happy path, no reflow jank on data arrival.

## Priority Issues

[P1] Font sizes fixed at 9–10px — fails WCAG 1.4.4 Resize Text
- today-badge: 9px. school-countdown-label/unit: 9px hard-coded. chip-weekday: clamp(9px, 2.3vw, 10px).
- Fix: Replace with rem units. 0.6875rem minimum for labels. clamp(0.625rem, 2.3vw, 0.75rem) for chip-weekday.
- Command: /impeccable audit

[P2] Glassmorphism on flat-dark backgrounds — decorative cost, no visual gain
- backdrop-filter: blur(8px) on entree-block, section-block, school-countdown.
- Dark mode surface resolves to ~rgb(24,24,31) — nothing interesting to blur.
- Fix: Remove backdrop-filter from all three. Card-bg token alone provides sufficient differentiation.
- Command: /impeccable optimize

[P2] Bounce easing on chip selection is off-brand
- chipSpring cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) — Y2=1.56 causes overshoot.
- Fix: Replace with cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1) (expo ease-out).
- Command: /impeccable animate

[P2] Section fade-in with opacity:0 start — blank-tab risk
- sectionFadeIn 0.3s ease both — both fill mode applies from{opacity:0} before animation ticks. Background-tab load leaves sections blank.
- Fix: Drop both fill mode; use forwards only. Or set from{opacity:0.01}.
- Command: /impeccable animate

[P3] "Day X of Y" counter is unexplained noise
- Students don't think in day indices. Chip strip already shows position.
- Fix: Remove or replace with "Week of Jun 9".
- Command: /impeccable clarify
